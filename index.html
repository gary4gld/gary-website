About Me

My name is Gary De la Cruz. I'm a person who appreciates learning and science. I love reading, anime, games, and writing. Respect, sincerity, and integrity are things I consider primordial in any interpersonal relationship.

I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, an associate degree in Computer Systems Engineering and Technology, and a minor in mathematics. I was a high school teacher for two years, teaching physics, engineering, and robotics. I created the engineering and robotics curriculum at the high school I worked at.

I am currently a junior in software engineering and this is the career path I want to keep following for now. I am also very interested in game design. I hope I can make a game of my own. Since I write, I have some stories I would like to show as games.
